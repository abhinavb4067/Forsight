Frequently Asked Questions – FIHM

1. What is the duration of the course?
The Diploma in Hotel Management at FIHM is a 1-year full-time program, including theory, practical training, and internship.
2. Is there a practical lab on campus?
Yes, FIHM features a state-of-the-art on-campus lab for food production, service, and housekeeping training.
3. Do you offer paid internships?
Yes, our students undergo a paid internship with reputed hotels and restaurants as part of the program.
4. Is hostel accommodation available?
Yes, hostel facilities are available for both male and female students near the institute.
5. Can students pay the fee in installments?
Yes. Also Education loan is available
6. What are the minimum eligibility criteria?
Should have passed 10th and attended 12th (pass or fail). No prior experience in hotel management is required. College dropouts and other backgrounds are also welcome.
7. Is there a certificate at the end of the program?
Yes, students receive a government-recognized diploma certificate suitable for national and international employment.
8. What is the medium of instruction?
Primarily English, with support in Malayalam and Hindi if needed.
9. How can I apply?
Apply online or directly at our office in Devagiri, Kozhikode. Seats are limited.
